      About Pac-Man

      Pac-Man is one of the most iconic arcade games of all time! In this legendary maze chase game, you guide Pac-Man through a labyrinth, gobbling up dots and fruits while avoiding the colorful ghosts that chase you. It's a perfect mix of speed, strategy, and nostalgia!

      How to Play Pac-Man

      Chomp all the dots

      Your main goal is to eat all the small yellow dots (called pellets) in the maze. Once you clear them all, you move to the next level.

      Avoid the ghosts

      Watch out for Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde — the four ghosts that roam the maze! If they catch you, you lose a life. Stay alert and plan your path carefully.

      Use power pellets

      Big flashing dots (power pellets) turn the tables! When you eat one, the ghosts become blue and vulnerable. Now you can chase and eat them for bonus points!

      Game Controls

      Mobile: Swipe in the direction you want Pac-Man to move.

      Desktop: Use the arrow keys to navigate the maze.

      Tips for High Scores

      • Memorize ghost movement patterns for better escape routes.
      • Save power pellets for when ghosts are close — maximize your ghost-chomping spree!
      • Always plan your next turn in advance to avoid traps.
      • Grab bonus fruits for extra points between levels.

      Game Features
